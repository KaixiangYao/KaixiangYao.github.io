About

I am a Robotics Engineering undergraduate at SUSTech and a visiting undergraduate researcher at UC Berkeley. My research interests are in robot learning, embodied AI, and manipulation, with an emphasis on reliable interaction with the physical world.

My previous work spans rigid-soft contact, physics-based digital twins, and assistive manipulation. I use simulation, mechanics, perception, and control to study these problems.

Research Interests

Robot learning, embodied AI, physical manipulation, contact-rich interaction, and assistive robotics.
