01 / ABOUT

About

My work examines how physical and task-level feedback enters robot learning and control. I use simulation, mechanics, perception, and system integration to test whether those signals are observable, identifiable, and useful for safer decisions.

I have applied this perspective to deformable-object manipulation, rigid–soft contact, robotic assembly, and assistive grasping at UC Berkeley, Shanghai Jiao Tong University, Tsinghua University, and SUSTech.
